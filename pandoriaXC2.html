Xenoblade Chronicles 2




Pandoria









In Xenoblade Chronicles 2, Pandoria is a Blade. She has the ability to give her Driver Zeke and his supporters a great deal of power. Pandoria is an Attacker in combat, employs the electric element, and has a Big Bang Edge.

As long as the Omega Fetter is not disrupted, Pandoria can communicate with and call upon the Titan Genbu, whom she has an inexplicable bond with, from beneath the Cloud Sea.
