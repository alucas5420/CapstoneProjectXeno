Xenoblade Chronicles 2




Mòrag









One of the primary playable characters in Xenoblade Chronicles 2 is Mòrag Ladair. She is the Empire of Mor Ardain's Special Inquisitor. She is a driver who travels with Brighid, her blade. Known as "the Flamebringer", she serves Emperor Niall, her biological cousin and adopted younger brother, and is regarded as the most powerful Driver in the Empire.

In Chapter 4, she joins the group during the conflict with Patroka and Mikhail. Mòrag arrives at the gathering wearing standard Spring Shoes.
