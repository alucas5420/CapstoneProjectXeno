Xenoblade Chronicles 1




Fiora










In Xenoblade Chronicles, Fiora is one of seven playable characters. She is the younger sister of Dunban, who she lives with in his home in Colony 9, and was a childhood companion of Shulk and Reyn. Shulk appreciates that she prepares for him frequently and has a warm spot for him. She joins Shulk and Reyn as playable characters in Chapter 1.
