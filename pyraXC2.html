Xenoblade Chronicles 2




Pyra










One of the female main characters in Xenoblade Chronicles 2 is Pyra. She is the Aegis, a Legendary Blade that can give her Driver, Rex, amazing fire-elemental power. Rex is committed to keeping Pyra safe as they set out on a quest to find Elysium. Because Pyra and Rex share a life energy, Pyra is just as susceptible to harm as any other human and cannot heal from wounds like other Blades. The death of one would result in the death of the other.
