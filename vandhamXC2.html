Xenoblade Chronicles 2




Vandham










Xenoblade Chronicles 2's Vandham was born Aquila Paronet Sol Esteriole. The Urayan Garfont Mercenaries are led by him. He serves as a mentor to Rex and the group while driving with his unique Blade, Roc.

Vandham joined the party at level 20 in Chapter 3 and left close to the end of the same chapter, making him a temporary member.
