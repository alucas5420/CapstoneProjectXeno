Xenoblade Chronicles 1




Riki










Riki is a member of the Nopon race and one of the seven playable characters in Xenoblade Chronicles. He is 40 years old, despite his demeanor, and six of his children live in Frontier Village. When the group gets to Frontier Village, he starts going with Shulk and his crew. It is in Chapter 7 that he becomes playable.
