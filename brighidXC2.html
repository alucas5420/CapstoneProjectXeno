Xenoblade Chronicles 2




Brighid










Brighid appears in Xenoblade Chronicles 2 as a Blade. She is the strongest Blade in the Empire and the Jewel of Mor Ardain. She has the ability to give her Driver Mòrag and her supporters a great deal of influence. Brighid is a Tank in combat, employs the element of fire, and wields Whipswords.
