Torna ~ The Golden Country




Addam










Xenoblade Chronicles 2 features Addam Origo. As the Driver of the Aegis, Mythra, during the Aegis War five hundred years before the game's events, he plays a crucial role in the game's backstory. In Torna ~ The Golden Country, he is a protagonist and a major playable character.
