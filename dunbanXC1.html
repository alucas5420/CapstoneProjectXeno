Xenoblade Chronicles 1




Dunban










One of Xenoblade Chronicles' seven playable characters, Dunban is a Homs. Dunban, who is prepared to kill a Homs to bring about peace, acts as the group's voice of reason. Dunban and his younger sister Fiora reside in a home he owns in Colony 9. He is first playable as a guest character in the Prologue and Chapter 2, and in Chapter 5, he becomes permanently playable.
