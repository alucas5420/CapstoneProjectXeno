Xenoblade Chronicles Future Redeemed




Rex










Xenoblade Chronicles 2's male protagonist and primary playable character is Rex. He is a Driver who uses the power of a Blade called Pyra, who is the fabled Aegis. Rex dedicates his life to keeping her safe, and he and Pyra set out to locate Elysium, her long-lost home. The dual nature of the Aegis allows Rex to use the power of Mythra, the weapon's other incarnation.
