Torna ~ The Golden Country




Minoth










His previous name was Minoth, and he is a Special Legendary Blade in Torna ~ The Golden Country. He has the ability to give Addam and his supporters enormous influence. Minoth is an Attacker in combat, uses the dark element, and carries Gunknifes.
