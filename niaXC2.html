Xenoblade Chronicles 2




Nia










One of the primary playable characters in Xenoblade Chronicles 2 is Nia. She is in the company of her Blade, Dromarch, who addresses her as "Lady" in a butler-like manner. Her home province is Gormott. She was once a part of Torna, but as Pyra awakens, she joins Rex.
